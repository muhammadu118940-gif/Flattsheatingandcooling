About Our IAQ Services

Healthier Air for Your Home & Business

The air inside your home can be significantly more polluted than outdoor air. Dust, allergens, mold spores, volatile organic compounds, and bacteria circulate through your HVAC system every day. Flatt's Heating & Cooling offers comprehensive indoor air quality solutions to help you breathe cleaner, healthier air.

We carry and install a full range of IAQ products including whole-home air purifiers, UV germicidal lights, HEPA filtration upgrades, humidifiers, dehumidifiers, and energy recovery ventilators. Our technicians will assess your home's needs and recommend the right solutions.

From allergy relief to odor elimination, better sleep to reduced sick days — improved indoor air quality makes a real difference in how your family feels every day.

What We Offer

Our IAQ Solutions

Air Purifiers

Whole-home air purifiers that remove up to 99.97% of airborne particles including dust, pollen, pet dander, mold spores, and bacteria.

UV Germicidal Lights

UV-C light systems installed in your ductwork kill mold, bacteria, and viruses as air passes through, protecting your whole family.

Humidifiers

Whole-home humidifiers maintain ideal moisture levels to prevent dry skin, static electricity, and damage to wood furniture and floors.

Dehumidifiers

Control excess moisture that leads to mold growth, musty odors, and structural damage with whole-home dehumidification systems.

HEPA Filtration

Upgrade your system's filtration to HEPA-grade or high-MERV media filters for dramatically improved particle capture efficiency.

Ventilation Systems

Energy recovery ventilators (ERVs) and heat recovery ventilators (HRVs) bring in fresh outdoor air without wasting conditioned air energy.

Signs You Need IAQ Help

Common Air Quality Warning Signs

01

Increased Allergy Symptoms

If household members are sneezing, coughing, or experiencing watery eyes more than usual indoors, poor air quality may be the culprit.

02

Musty or Stale Odors

Persistent musty smells often indicate mold or mildew growth in your ductwork or HVAC components that needs immediate attention.

03

Excessive Dust Buildup

If surfaces dust up quickly after cleaning, your filtration system may not be capturing enough particles from the circulating air.

04

Dry Skin & Static Electricity

Low humidity levels during winter months cause dry skin, chapped lips, and annoying static. A whole-home humidifier resolves this.

05

Condensation on Windows

Excessive condensation indicates high humidity levels that promote mold growth. A dehumidifier can bring moisture back to healthy levels.

06

Frequent Illness in the Home

If family members get sick often, airborne bacteria and viruses circulating through your HVAC system may be the source.
