Warning Signs

Signs Your Water Heater Needs Service

01

No Hot Water

If you're running out of hot water quickly or have no hot water at all, your water heater may have a failed heating element or faulty thermostat.

02

Rusty or Discolored Water

Rust-colored water coming from your hot water taps indicates corrosion inside the tank — a sign the unit may need replacement soon.

03

Rumbling or Popping Sounds

Loud noises from your water heater are caused by sediment buildup on the heating element — an annual flush can prevent this.

04

Leaking Water

Any water pooling around your water heater is a serious issue that needs immediate attention to prevent water damage to your home.

05

Age Over 10 Years

Most traditional water heaters last 8–12 years. If yours is approaching or past this range, consider a proactive replacement to avoid unexpected failure.

06

Rising Energy Bills

A water heater that's working harder than it should due to sediment or component wear will show up as higher gas or electric bills.
