Our History

BUILT ON TRUST
& EXPERTISE

Flatt's Heating & Cooling was founded by Joey Flatt in 2009 with a simple mission: deliver honest, expert HVAC service to the Sparta community at a fair price. What started as a one-man operation has grown into a trusted team serving eight cities across Middle Tennessee.

With over 25 years of combined industry experience across our technician team, we've seen — and fixed — just about everything. From aging systems that need expert repair to new construction installs requiring precise engineering, our crew handles every job with the same care and professionalism.

We believe in using only quality parts, standing behind our work with comprehensive warranties, and treating every customer's home the way we'd treat our own. That's not just a tagline — it's how we've earned the trust of thousands of families across Sparta, Cookeville, Crossville, and beyond.

What We Stand For

Our Mission & Values

Quality Parts
We never cut corners. Every component we install — from capacitors to compressors — is sourced from trusted manufacturers including Goodman, Trane, Rheem, and Generac. Quality parts mean fewer breakdowns and longer system life.
Expert Technicians
Our licensed and certified technicians bring decades of hands-on experience to every job. We stay current with the latest HVAC technologies through continuous training, so you always get service that meets or exceeds industry standards.
24/7 Availability
HVAC emergencies don't follow business hours, and neither do we. Our team is available around the clock, 365 days a year. When your heat goes out in January or your AC fails in August, one call is all it takes to get help on the way.
